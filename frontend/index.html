The History Of Our Honor Society

UPE is the first and only, existing international honor society in the Computing and Information disciplines. UPE was first organized at Texas A&M University, College Station, Texas, in 1967. The international organization now consists of over 300 chapters in various colleges and universities in North America and overseas...
